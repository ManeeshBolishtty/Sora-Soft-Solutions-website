About Us

Why the Name SORASOFT

The name SORASOFT SOLUTIONS reflects how we think about technology and business.

“Sora” means sky in Japanese. For us, it represents clarity, openness, and a long-term view. Enterprise systems are complex by nature, but they don’t have to be confusing. We chose this name to reflect our belief that software should bring structure and visibility, not noise.

“Soft” stands for our core strength — software and SAP expertise — while “Solutions” reflects our focus on solving real business problems, not just implementing systems.

The name SORASOFT Solutions is intentional. It represents clear thinking, disciplined execution, and technology built to scale.

Who We Are

SORASOFT Solutions is a specialized SAP consulting firm focused on delivering reliable, enterprise-grade solutions. We work primarily with SAP Vistex and SAP S/4HANA, helping organizations manage pricing, rebates, incentives, and complex commercial processes with accuracy and control. Our services cover consulting, implementation, and long-term support.

What sets us apart is not just technical capability, but how we work — structured, transparent, and accountable. We take the time to understand business realities and design solutions that are practical, scalable, and easy to operate.

Our Services

SAP Consulting & Advisory

We help organizations translate commercial complexity into clear, executable SAP designs. Our advisory work focuses on Vistex and S/4HANA, covering pricing models, rebate and incentive programs, contract logic, and the governance required to keep them accurate and auditable.

We work closely with business and IT teams to define a practical roadmap with clear trade-offs, timelines, and measurable business value. Our deliverables include process maps, Vistex solution blueprints, S/4HANA impact analysis, and governance rules that make pricing decisions auditable.

Implementation & Migration

We deliver end-to-end implementations and migrations that move revenue operations from spreadsheets and legacy systems into stable, scalable Vistex and S/4HANA environments.

Our approach is phased and test-driven, with early validation of data and integrations to ensure predictable go-lives and minimal business disruption. Deliverables include migration scripts, integration adapters, test suites, and cutover playbooks.

Support & Managed Services

We provide proactive, SLA-backed support and managed services to keep Vistex and S/4HANA landscapes stable, secure, and continuously improving.

Beyond issue resolution, we focus on system health, performance, and ongoing alignment with evolving commercial requirements. We provide named support pods, proactive health checks, release management, and a continuous improvement backlog.
